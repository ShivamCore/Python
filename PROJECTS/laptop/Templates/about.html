About Laptop Price Predictor

The Laptop Price Predictor project uses machine learning to estimate the resale price of laptops based on specifications such as brand, processor type, RAM, storage, and graphics card.
By training on a cleaned dataset (clean_lptp.csv), the model can provide an estimated value for used laptops.

This tool helps users make informed decisions when buying or selling laptops by offering a price prediction grounded in real-world data and AI technology.
